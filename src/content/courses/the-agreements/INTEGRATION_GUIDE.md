THE CURE and THE AGREEMENTS are two complementary pathways to the same destination: **freedom from mental suffering and the ability to live authentically.**

- **THE AGREEMENTS** teaches you *how to live consciously* (the practice of truth)

Together, they form a complete transformation system:
- Understanding + Application

This guide explains how the two courses integrate, which to take first, and how to use them together for maximum transformation.

**Key Teachings:**

- You feel your thinking, not your circumstances

- Depression is a misunderstanding of how mind works

**Outcome:** Deep understanding that changes your relationship to your thoughts and emotions

### THE AGREEMENTS: Applying the Understanding
**Based on:** The Four Agreements + The Fifth Agreement (Toltec wisdom)
**Core Insight:** You've been domesticated to believe agreements that create suffering
**Primary Focus:** Practicing conscious living through specific agreements

**Key Teachings:**
- Domestication created a false "Book of Law" you live by
- The Judge/Victim (Parasite) maintains suffering through broken agreements
- Conscious agreements replace unconscious conditioning
- Living impeccably creates personal freedom

**Outcome:** Daily practices that embody understanding in every relationship and situation

## Which Should You Take First?

### Option 1: THE CURE First (Recommended for most)

**Start with THE CURE if:**
- You're suffering from depression, anxiety, or persistent negative thinking
- You want to understand WHY you feel the way you feel
- You're new to consciousness work and need a foundation
- You're skeptical and want the neuroscience/psychology backing

**Then take THE AGREEMENTS to:**
- Apply your understanding in daily life
- Transform specific relationships
- Build sustainable practices
- Embody freedom in action

**Why this order works:**
When you understand how Thought creates experience (THE CURE), the agreements make immediate sense. You see WHY gossip creates suffering (it reinforces negative thought). You see WHY taking things personally hurts (you're treating others' thoughts as your reality).

---

### Option 2: THE AGREEMENTS First

**Start with THE AGREEMENTS if:**
- You're ready to change how you show up in relationships NOW
- You resonate with the Toltec wisdom tradition
- You prefer practice-based learning over theory
- You already have some understanding of how mind works

**Then take THE CURE to:**
- Understand the psychological/spiritual mechanics beneath the agreements
- Deepen your insight into why the practices work
- Address depression or persistent thought patterns
- Integrate modern neuroscience with ancient wisdom

**Why this order works:**
Some people learn best by DOING first, understanding second. Practicing the agreements reveals insights about Thought naturally. You notice: "When I don't take things personally, my thinking changes." Then THE CURE explains WHY.

---

### Option 3: Take Them Simultaneously

**Take both at once if:**
- You're highly motivated and have 4-6 hours/week for coursework
- You learn best through multiple perspectives at once
- You're in crisis and need both understanding AND immediate practices
- You want to accelerate your transformation

**How to do this:**
- Alternate weeks: One week focus on THE CURE, next week focus on THE AGREEMENTS
- Morning/Evening split: THE CURE lessons in morning, THE AGREEMENTS practices in evening
- Parallel processing: Apply agreements during the week, integrate insights from THE CURE in weekly reflection

**Why this works:**
The courses reinforce each other. THE CURE gives you the "why," THE AGREEMENTS gives you the "how." When you understand both simultaneously, transformation accelerates.
